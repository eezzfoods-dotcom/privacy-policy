# Privacy Policy — Imposter India

**Last updated: June 2026**

## Overview

Imposter India ("the App") is a multiplayer party game developed by Eezzfoods. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

The App does not require account creation, login, or any personal details. When you play online, you enter a display name of your choice — this name is only used within your game session and is not stored anywhere after the game ends.

## Game Session Data

During online gameplay, temporary room data (player names, game state) is stored in memory on our servers for the duration of your game session. This data is automatically deleted when the game ends or after 3 hours of inactivity.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- No real names
- No email addresses
- No phone numbers
- No location data
- No device identifiers
- No usage analytics
- No cookies

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

- **Socket.io / Node.js** — for real-time multiplayer communication. No personal data is transmitted.
- **Vercel** — frontend hosting. Standard server logs may be created but contain no personal data.
- **Render** — backend hosting. Standard server logs may be created but contain no personal data.

## Children's Privacy

This App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we collect no personal information at all, there is no risk to children's privacy.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
